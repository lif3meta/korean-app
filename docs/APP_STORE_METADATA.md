## Review Notes (paste into App Store Connect -> App Review Information -> Notes)

To test AI Voice Chat (requires microphone):
1. Open the app -> tap "Chat with a Teacher"
2. Select either teacher (Minji or Junwoo)
3. Choose "Voice Chat"
4. Grant microphone permission when prompted
5. Speak in English or attempt Korean — the AI teacher responds with audio in real time
6. Tap the mic button again when done speaking (a "Tap to stop" hint appears)
7. The teacher speaks Korean with native pronunciation and English for explanations

To test Speech Practice (requires microphone):
1. Open any pronunciation lesson or sentence practice
2. Tap the microphone button
3. Grant microphone permission when prompted
4. Speak the Korean phrase shown
5. Tap the stop button when done
6. Listen to your recording and self-assess

To test AI Text Chat:
Same steps as voice chat but choose "Text Chat" instead. Type in English or Korean.

To test Text-to-Speech audio (no mic required):
Tap any vocabulary word, sentence example, or reading passage — audio plays native Korean pronunciation via the speaker icon.

Notes:
- Voice chat requires an active internet connection for real-time AI audio (Google Gemini Live API).
- Text-to-speech uses a server-side TTS proxy for natural Korean audio.
- Microphone is used ONLY for voice chat with AI tutors and speech practice — no audio is stored or transmitted beyond the real-time session.
- No login required. The app works immediately on launch.
